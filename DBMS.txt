	Ellipses : Attributes

	Diamonds : Relationship

	Lines : Link b/w Attributes, Entities & Relationships

                Double Ellipses : Multi Valued Attributes

	Dashed Ellipses : Derived Attributes

	Double Lines : Total Participation
